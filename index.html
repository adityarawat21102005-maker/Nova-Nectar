FAQ

1. What are the different types of coffee beans?

Arabica: Smooth, complex flavor.
Robusta: Strong, bitter.
Liberica:Fruity, floral.
Excelsa:Tart, dark.

2. How should I store my coffee beans to keep them fresh?

Store in an airtight container in a cool, dark place. Avoid air, moisture, heat, and light.

3. What’s the difference between espresso and regular coffee?

Espresso is concentrated and strong, made with high pressure. Regular coffee is milder, made by dripping hot water over grounds.

4. How can I make the perfect cup of coffee at home?

Use freshly ground beans, measure accurately, use filtered water, maintain the right temperature (195-205°F), and brew for 4-5 minutes.

5. What are the health benefits of drinking coffee?

Coffee may enhance cognitive function, provide antioxidants, reduce disease risk, improve physical performance, and boost mood.
